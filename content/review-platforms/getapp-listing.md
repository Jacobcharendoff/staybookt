# GrowthOS — GetApp Listing

## Product Overview

GrowthOS is a CRM and field service management platform built for Canadian trades. We help contractors manage their entire operation: leads, jobs, scheduling, invoicing, and payments. One platform. Less chaos.

Stop losing leads to the company that responds first. Stop calculating taxes by hand. Stop wondering if you're leaving money on the table. GrowthOS plugs those leaks.

Built in Canada for Canadian contractors. Full support for all provinces. Bilingual. Mobile app that works in the field. Tax calculations that are actually correct.

## Core Features

- **Lead Management:** Capture leads from Google, Facebook, website forms, and calls. Centralize everything. Get real-time alerts. Set up automatic follow-ups.

- **Job Scheduling:** Visual calendar. Assign jobs to the right person. Optimize routes. Send job details and time windows to customers. Accept online bookings.

- **Automatic Tax Handling:** GST, HST, PST, QST—all calculated correctly per province. No manual math. No billing errors. Compliance baked in.

- **Mobile App:** Full-featured phone app. View jobs, update status, take photos, collect signatures, request payment. Works offline. Designed for contractors working in the field.

- **Invoicing & Payments:** Auto-generate invoices. Process payment on-site or send digital invoices. Track what's paid, pending, or overdue. Integration with Canadian payment processors.

- **Crew & Team Management:** Assign jobs to crews. See real-time GPS location. Monitor job progress. Manage availability and time off.

- **Customer Database:** Every customer has a complete profile. Service history, equipment details, past quotes, payment records. Your team has context.

- **Bilingual Interface:** English and French built in. Communicate with customers in their language. No workarounds.
