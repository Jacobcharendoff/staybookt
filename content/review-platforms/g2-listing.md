# GrowthOS — G2 Listing

## Product Overview

GrowthOS is a field service CRM built specifically for Canadian trades. We help plumbers, HVAC contractors, electricians, landscapers, and roofers manage leads, jobs, and customers in one place. Stop losing leads. Stop doing tax math by hand. Stop using spreadsheets to track jobs. GrowthOS does all of that automatically.

We handle Canadian taxes (GST, HST, PST, QST) natively. We support English and French. We work on mobile phones—even when you're offline in the field. And we don't charge per user, so you can give your whole team access without breaking the budget.

Most field service companies leave money on the table every month because leads fall through the cracks and jobs aren't tracked properly. GrowthOS plugs those leaks. Catch every lead. Respond faster. Schedule smarter. Get paid on time.

## Key Features

- **Lead Management & Automation:** Capture leads from Google, Facebook, your website, and phone calls in one place. Automatic notifications alert your team in real-time. Set up follow-up sequences so nothing goes silent.

- **Job Scheduling & Dispatch:** Schedule jobs on a shared calendar. Dispatch to the right team member based on location, skills, and availability. Track job progress in real-time with GPS and photo updates from the field.

- **Canadian Tax Engine:** Automatic tax calculations for every province. GST, HST, PST, QST—all handled automatically. Invoices are tax-correct from day one. No more manual recalculation.

- **Mobile App:** Full CRM access on your phone. View jobs, update status, take photos, get paid. Works offline. Your team works in the field, not the office.

- **Invoicing & Payments:** Generate invoices automatically from job details. Collect payments on the spot or send invoices to customers. Payment tracking shows what's paid, what's pending, what's overdue.

- **Bilingual Support:** English and French interface. Communicate with customers in their language. Especially valuable for Quebec and bilingual markets across Canada.

## Target Audience

GrowthOS is built for:
- Small to mid-size trades (1–30 people)
- Residential service companies (plumbing, HVAC, electrical, etc.)
- Canadian contractors who need tax compliance built in
- Team leaders who want visibility without micro-managing
- Owners tired of spreadsheets and scattered information

If you're running a trades business in Canada and everything is scattered between email, text, Facebook, and your head, GrowthOS is for you.
