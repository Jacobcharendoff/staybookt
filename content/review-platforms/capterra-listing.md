# Staybookt — Capterra Listing

## Product Overview

Staybookt is a field service CRM designed for Canadian contractors. We help trades manage the full job lifecycle: capturing leads, scheduling jobs, tracking progress, and getting paid. Everything runs on one platform—no more juggling email, spreadsheets, and phone calls.

Stop leaving money on the table. Catch every lead. Respond first. Get the job. Get paid fast.

Whether you're a plumber in Ontario, an HVAC company in Quebec, or an electrician in BC, Staybookt handles your taxes automatically, supports your language, and works on your team's phones.

## Key Features

- **Lead Capture & Management:** All your lead sources (Google Local Services, Facebook, website forms, phone calls) flow into one inbox. Real-time alerts ensure your team responds before the competition.

- **Intelligent Scheduling:** Drag-and-drop scheduling across your team. Assign jobs based on location, skill, and availability. Customers see available time slots and book themselves.

- **Automatic Tax Calculations:** GST, HST, PST, QST—all calculated correctly based on province and job type. No manual work. No errors. Compliance built in.

- **Mobile-First Field App:** Your team works in the field. The app works offline. View jobs, mark completion, take before-and-after photos, collect signatures, request payment—all from the phone.

- **Invoice & Payment Processing:** Auto-generate invoices from job details. Process payments on-site or send invoices digitally. Track payment status in real-time.

- **Bilingual Interface:** Native English and French support. Serve customers in their preferred language. Essential for Quebec; valuable everywhere.

- **Crew Management:** Assign jobs to crews. Track which jobs they're working on. See real-time location with GPS. Know exactly who is where and what they're doing.

- **Customer History:** Every customer has a profile. Past service dates, equipment details, previous quotes, payment history. Your team has context before they arrive at the job.
